## English (English)

### About this App
This is a mental care chat app where you can gently release your daily anxieties and inner turmoil.
Like a professional counselor, the AI focuses on accepting your feelings kindly without judgment.

### Features
- **Non-judgmental Empathy**: The AI warmly accepts your words.
- **Privacy First**: Your entries are saved only in your browser (LocalStorage) and are never sent to a server.
- **Easy Reset**: You can clear your records at any time.
